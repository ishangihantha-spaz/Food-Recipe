Pan-Seared Ribeye Steak with Garlic Butter

By Chef John
25 mins
650 kcal
(128 Reviews)

Description

This pan-seared ribeye steak is the ultimate steakhouse experience right in your kitchen. Basted with garlic-herb butter, it’s juicy, tender, and incredibly flavorful. Perfect for a special dinner or simply when you want to treat yourself.

Ingredients

  • 2 (1-inch thick) ribeye steaks
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

1

Prep the Steaks

Let the steaks sit at room temperature for 30 minutes. Pat dry with paper towels. Season generously with salt and pepper on all sides.

2

Sear

Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat. Add oil and heat until shimmering. Carefully place steaks in the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes until a brown crust forms. Flip.

3

Baste with Butter

Reduce heat to medium. Add butter, garlic, and rosemary. Tilt the pan and spoon the foaming butter over the steaks repeatedly for 1-2 minutes until desired doneness (130°F for medium-rare).

4

Rest

Remove steaks from pan and let them rest on a board for 5-10 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute.
